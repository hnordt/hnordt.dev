# AI

> This page is a temporary draft while I finish the full essay.

I use AI as a practical extension of product engineering: faster exploration,
clearer tradeoffs, and more disciplined execution.

The useful work is not prompting for novelty. It is turning rough product intent
into systems that can be inspected, tested, shipped, and maintained.

## Where it helps

- Shaping early product decisions into concrete implementation paths.
- Moving across design, frontend, backend, testing, and deployment without
  losing context.
- Reviewing edge cases and failure modes before they become production issues.
- Automating repetitive engineering work while keeping the important decisions
  explicit.

## Where judgment still matters

AI raises the ceiling only when the surrounding process is strong. Architecture,
product taste, security boundaries, data modeling, and release quality still need
senior ownership.

The goal is not to replace engineering judgment. The goal is to make that
judgment show up earlier, more often, and with less friction.
